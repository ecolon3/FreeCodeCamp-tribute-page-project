Tia Kofi
The Question Remains!

Tia started RuPaul's Drag Race on an inspired note, choosing an Alan Turing-themed look for the category "UK Gay Icons." Experts estimate that Turing's revolutionary codebreaking for British intelligence during the Second World War saved over 14 million lives. Nonetheless, Turing later faced conviction and even forced estrogen injections because he acknowledged being in a gay relationship while reporting a burglary of his home.

Tia Kofi is the stage name of the drag queen Lawrence Bolton, who appeared on the second season of RuPaul's Drag Race UK in 2021. The name is a play on the question, "tea or coffee?" While on the show, Tia survived two lip sync battles before becoming the seventh queen eliminated. She may not have brought what the judges were looking for this time, but her charming personality and self-deprecating humor made a big impact on fans.

Fun facts about Tia:

  • As Lawrence, Tia appeared in the 2004 movie "Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban."
  • On RuPaul's Drag Race UK Season 2, Tia also appeared as a pterodactyl, an ice cream cone, Robin Hood, and Spice Girl Mel B.
  • Tia stands nearly 6 feet, 5 inches tall. And even taller in heels!
